PERT Team Calculator

Estimate story points for your team using PERT — with blocker buffers.

PERT = (O + 4M + P) / 6
Blocked = PERT × 1.20
Total SP = sum of all members
O = Optimistic days (best case)  ·  M = Most likely days  ·  P = Pessimistic days (worst case)
The PERT estimate weighs the most likely scenario 4× heavier than the extremes, giving a realistic weighted average. If a task has a blocker, a 20% buffer is added to the PERT estimate before mapping to story points. Story points are then mapped using the standard Fibonacci-like scale in the reference table below.
